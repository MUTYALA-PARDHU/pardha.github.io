About Me

I'm a research scholar at IIT Gandhinagar, working in the areas of combustion and computational fluid dynamics (CFD). My work focuses on multiphase combustion, thermo-transport processes, and numerical methods. I'm especially interested in using simulations to better understand how combustion works by diving deep into the underlying physics.
